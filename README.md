## 📁 Files in This Repository

### 1. `ai_credit_survey_multilingual.html`
**AI Credit Scoring & Financial Inclusion — Pilot Survey**
A multilingual online survey instrument developed as part of a PhD research study at Lincoln University, New Zealand, examining how AI-driven credit scoring affects financial inclusion across nine developing economies.

- Available in 6 languages: English, Portuguese, Spanish, Bahasa Malaysia, Vietnamese, and Bengali
- Covers 6 survey sections: Consent, Financial Access, AI Awareness, Regulation, Financial Literacy, and Impact
- All responses are recorded in English regardless of language used
- Supervised by Professor Christopher Gan, Lincoln University

🔗 **Live Survey:** [Click here to take the survey](https://chinelouni.github.io/Online-survey-AI-Driven-Credit-Scoring-and-Financial-Inclusion-/ai_credit_survey_multilingual.html)

### 2. `OLOTP_Executive_Presentation.html`
**Parish Survey — Executive Leadership Presentation**
An interactive data-driven presentation translating the OLOTP parishioner survey findings (n = 42) into six strategic priorities for parish leadership.

**Six Strategic Priorities covered:**
1. 🌍 **Grow Through Evangelisation** — Increase invitation, outreach, and newcomer integration
2. 📖 **Deepen Faith Formation** — Strengthen prayer, discipleship, and spiritual growth beyond Sunday Mass
3. 🌱 **Develop Future Parish Leaders** — Expand volunteer engagement and leadership succession
4. 👨‍👩‍👧 **Invest in Families and Young People** — Strengthen parish-school-family partnerships
5. 🤝 **Build Community and Belonging** — Create stronger relationships across all age groups and cultures
6. 💝 **Strengthen Stewardship** — Mobilise time, talent, and treasure for sustainable parish mission

🔗 **Live Presentation:** [Click here to view the presentation](https://chinelouni.github.io/Online-survey-AI-Driven-Credit-Scoring-and-Financial-Inclusion-/OLOTP_Executive_Presentation.html)
